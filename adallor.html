Why do you need a replaceable watch band?

A nice watch band is as important as the watch.
If wearing a watch has become your lifestyle, then you may hold this fantasy just like us: we hope my watch strap can meet all our needs in every situation.
In the winter, we want a comfortabable watch strap to bring us warmth. In the summer, we want the strap to be waterproof and make us feel light.
When life is illuminated, we hope the strap can keep us calm down and make us realize the weight of life; when life is down, we hope the strap is colorful, which will give us power to embrace the world.

Shopping with Adallor®, we can meet all above demands!
We provide a choice from among various different materials and designs: silicone, cloth, metal, nylon, camouflage, etc. Most of our straps are quick release designed, each style of model can be quickly and easily detached.
